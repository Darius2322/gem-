Our Story

Founded in 2012, LUMIÃˆRE has grown from a small boutique in Nairobi to Africa's premier luxury destination. Our name, meaning "light" in French, reflects our mission to illuminate beauty and elegance in every product we offer.

What began as a passion project for founder Amina Kiprop has evolved into a brand that celebrates African craftsmanship while embracing global luxury standards. Each piece in our collection tells a story of dedication, quality, and timeless elegance.

Our Journey

2012

Humble Beginnings

Founded as a small boutique in Nairobi's Westlands district, focusing on locally crafted jewelry.

2015

About LUMIÈRE

Our Story

Founded in 2012, LUMIÈRE has grown from a small boutique in Nairobi to Africa's premier luxury destination. Our name, meaning "light" in French, reflects our mission to illuminate beauty and elegance in every product we offer.

Our Philosophy

We believe luxury should be accessible, ethical, and sustainable. Every piece in our collection is carefully curated, ensuring exceptional quality while supporting local artisans and sustainable practices.
